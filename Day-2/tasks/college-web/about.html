About DMI College of Engineering

DMI College of Engineering (DMICE) is a renowned institution located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. The college is committed to delivering quality technical education and nurturing future engineers with strong academic foundations, ethical values, and innovative thinking.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality education and research opportunities in engineering and technology, fostering innovation and excellence in our students and faculty.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in engineering education and research, contributing to the development of society and the advancement of technology.
